Actollo Fitness

Jul. 2018

The biggest problem people have at the gym is figuring out what to do there. To eliviate the stress of looking clueless or forgetting the workout regimen, Actollo Fitness takes care of it for the athlete. To understand the pattern in Japan's uniquely decreasing population, I utilized Python along with matplotlib and Pandas. Hardware

Autonomous Racing Car

Jan. 2017

With the Arduino micrcontroller and ArduinoC, I designed and built an autonomous car. By using LDR (Light Dependent Resistors) as the sensor that keeps the car within the lanes outlined by black markers, the car is able to navigate itself. The operation was given a time constraint of 2 minutes which was satisfied. This project required a combination of software and hands-on hardware skills. Tasks like wiring, soldering, and building from raw materials was common.

Image Replicator

Nov. 2018

Our team was given the task to build a robot capable of replicating an image by using a plotting system. With the LEGO MINDSTORM platoform, Tetrix Kit, RobotC, and 3D-printed components, we were able to complete the task. By accepting a Boolean 2D array that stores the outline of the processed image's object, the robot is capable of reproducing images to a 160 x 400-pixel quality.

VEX Robotics

Feb. 2017

To compete in the International VEX Robotics Competition, I was given the opportunity to build a robot with a team to fulfill the competition objective. The robot was remotely controllable, and competed in a ring with other teams. My specific role in the team was to build the mechanical components that elevated the robot and the objective pylons.
